Milkshake!

Let's make a milkshake!

Ingredients

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Milk

Optional:

  • Maraschino Cherry
  • Your favorite Chocolate
  • Sprinkles
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. First, insert 4 scoops of Vanilla Ice Cream and 1 cup of milk into a blender.
  2. Next, insert your favorite chocolate into the blender as well
  3. Blend all together until preferred viscocity
  4. Lastly, Top it off with whipped cream, sprinkles and your cherry!
  5. ENJOY!!
