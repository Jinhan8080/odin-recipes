Pizza!

Let's make some pizza!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough of your choice
  • Tomato or alfredo sauce or sauce of your choice
  • Toppings (Peperoni, Onion, Green Pepper, Bacon, Chicken, Sausage, Etc)
  • Mozarella cheese or cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Let's preheat the oven to 450 °F first
  2. Next, spread the dough out into pizza shape by pushing it out or throwing it up in the air like you see in movies
  3. Place the tomato sauce or sauce of your choice on the flattened out dough
  4. Let's lay down some cheese first and other toppings you want to put on the pizza, no judgement zone here
  5. Place the prepared pizza in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes depending on how long you want to cook it for
  6. There you go! Enjoy your pizza!
