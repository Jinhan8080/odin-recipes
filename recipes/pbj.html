Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich!

Today, I am going to show you how to make a peanut butter jelly sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly of your choice
  • Bread of your choice

Steps

  1. First, toast your bread to your liking.
  2. Second, spread the peanut butter on one side of the bread, and spread the jelly of your choice to the other side
  3. Lastly, combine the two breads together to make your perfect sandwich
  4. If you want to make it even better, heat up the pan and use butter to grill the sandwich for a buttery, melted delicious sandwich!
